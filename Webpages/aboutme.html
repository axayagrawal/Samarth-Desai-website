Samarth Desai is a well known Hindustani Classical Guitarist and Composer. He is A National Award Winner In National Youth Festival Punjab, And 5 State Award. He Started his music Journey under his father’s guidance and then went on to learn classical music from Pt.Mallikarjun Taralgatti.

There on he created his unique Hindustani classical style which led him to showcase his talent in shows like INDIA’S GOT TALENT with tHE PUNCH BAND. He has performed with legendary artists like Pt.Jayatheerth Mevundi, Hariharan, Roopkumar Rathod and many. Also performed across the whole country and abroad in Singapore, Thailand, etc
