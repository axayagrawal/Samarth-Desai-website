Beginner’s Curriculam.

  • Grasp the essentials needed to begin playing acoustic or electric guitar.
  • You'll learn an easy approach to get you playing quickly, through a combination of exploring the instrument, performance technique, and basic music theory.
  • The course begins simply with the parts of the guitar, the names of the strings, tuning, and technique—whether finger-style or pick. It then explores the basics of music theory with such topics as scales, triads, power chords, and fingering and shapes.
  • They will have the foundational knowledge necessary to pursue most intermediate guitar courses.

About Guru Shishya

  • Classes are open to all.
  • Duration of each class will be 30 minutes.
  • Students will have to take prior appointment by sending an email to Pravinji at pravingodkhindi@gmail.com
  • The day and time of the classes will be decided by Pravinji and the student by mutual convenience.
  • Students are requested to send in their appointment/request emails at least 1 week prior to the date and time of the class required.
  • Classes are being conducted online through SKYPE. Students are expected to download the required software themselves. Hi-speed internet connection, a good head-set with microphone and a silent environment, are some of the other conditions students are expected to take care of.
  • We are not responsible if ongoing classes are disrupted in between due to unforeseen circumstances. In such cases classes can be rescheduled.
  • Although these classes are designed to be one-on-one, Pravinji has the right to include any number of students through a group/conference call.
  • Audio/video recording, publishing of any Audio/Video/text material thereof is strictly prohibited and is deemed violation of copyrights.
